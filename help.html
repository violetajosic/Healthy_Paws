Home page

On this page you can type catalog ID that you got from a veterinarian clinics, and open it.

Diseases page

On this page you can check symptoms your pet have and search possible diseases by clicking on FIND EXAMPLES button.

Make new catalog page

On this page clinic can make new pet catalog, upload an owner and pet image, pet name and owner full name breed of the pet and its age both as an animal and human.
Clicking on MAKE CATALOG button the catalog with its ID would be made.

Profile page

On this page you as a client can see your data - email, password, catalog ID number, membership start and expiring date, as well as the option to continue with membership by clicking on PAY MEMBERSHIP FEE button.
